CPU-IF
System Information Software
What's new ?

CPU-IF is freeware that collects information about your system's main devices such as processor, motherboard, ram, graphics card, operating system and storage.

You can also use CPU-IF to check the suitability of your device for the installation of Windows 11.

Minimum OS
Windows 7
